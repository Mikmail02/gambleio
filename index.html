AI Music Studio, music player & fixes

🎵 Global Music Player

A persistent music player is now available across the entire site.

  • Floating player — A compact music player appears on every page with play/pause, skip, and volume controls.
  • Drag to move — Click and drag the player anywhere on your screen. Your position is remembered between sessions.
  • Seek bar — Scrub through tracks with a visual progress bar showing elapsed and remaining time.
  • Favorites — Heart any track to add it to your favorites playlist. Switch between "All Songs" and "Favorites" with the playlist toggle.
  • Sequential playback — Tracks play in order and auto-advances to the next song when one ends.
  • Owner music management — Site owners can upload and delete tracks directly from the Admin panel.

🎙️ AI Music Studio

Generate original music tracks using AI — right inside the site.

  • Create tab — Describe a track or write your own lyrics and let AI compose and sing it for you. Choose your genre, style, and model.
  • Simple & Advanced modes — Simple mode takes a single description. Advanced mode lets you set the title, style, and lyrics separately.
  • Music video generation — Optionally request a full music video alongside your track. The video syncs and loops with your audio in the player.
  • My Songs — All tracks you generate are saved to your personal library with live status updates while they generate.
  • Publish to All Songs — Completed tracks can be published to the global library for every player on the site to hear.
  • Synced lyrics — If a track has lyrics, they appear in a panel that highlights the current line in real time as the song plays.
  • All Songs — Browse every published AI track from all players in one place, directly from the music player.

🐛 Bug fixes

  • Profile pages — Visiting another player's profile no longer shows broken unstyled HTML.
  • Blackjack payouts — Push now correctly returns your bet. No more double-deduction on losses.
  • Blackjack balance timing — Balance no longer updates before the result is shown.
  • Case Battle stats — Wins and losses from Case Battles now count on the leaderboard and live stats.
  • Case Battle overview — Battle cards now show the entry price per player instead of the total pot.

🚿 Golden Shower — major overhaul

A comprehensive update to Golden Shower based on playtesting. Animations, buys, stats, layout, audio and routing all improved.

  • Mystery Bonus teaser — Buying the Lucky Draw triggers a dramatic column-by-column drop that reveals each reel one at a time before showing your spin count. Regular buys with 2+ BONUS symbols also trigger the teaser.
  • Bonus tracker panel — A side panel shows your remaining Gold Spins with a live counter, animated dots and a progress bar. It only appears once you press Start — no spoilers before the reveal.
  • Bonus re-triggers — Landing a BONUS symbol during active Gold Spins awards +1 extra free spin. A floating "+1" pops up from the exact cell so you don't miss it.
  • No spoilers — Your balance and live stats no longer update until the spin's cascade has fully finished animating. During Gold Spins your balance updates after each individual free spin so you can watch your wins grow.
  • Animation lock — The bonus intro modal is now guaranteed to never appear before all symbols have finished landing on the board, regardless of which feature mode triggered the bonus.
  • Session stats — Spins, XP gained, total wagered, total won, profit/loss and best win are all tracked for your current session and reset when you close the game.
  • Live stats math fix — The wagered and profit stats now correctly reflect the full cost of a bonus buy instead of just the base bet.
  • Global layout — Golden Shower now runs inside the main casino layout so the navigation header and chat are always visible. The slot scales cleanly within the content area.
  • Refresh-proof — Refreshing the page while playing Golden Shower keeps the header and chat visible and reloads the game in place.
  • Full-bleed background — The slot's background now fills the entire content area with no colour bleed from the site background at the edges.
  • Layout alignment — The stats bar, controls bar and game grid now share a single consistent width so all edges line up perfectly.

  • Wild Burst fix — Wild Burst explosions no longer destroy BONUS, STEAM, DRAIN or FLOATER symbols.

Golden Shower slot launch

🚿 New game: Golden Shower

A brand-new slot machine with a unique cluster-pays system — no paylines, just groups of 5 or more matching symbols anywhere on the 6×5 grid. Symbols explode and new ones fall in, letting you chain multiple wins in a single spin. Every cell on the board has its own multiplier that upgrades as wins hit that position — getting up to ×999 on the hottest spots.

  • Cluster pays — Connect 5 or more matching symbols to win; bigger clusters pay more
  • Cascading wins — Winning symbols disappear and new ones drop in, potentially triggering another win in the same spin
  • Position multipliers — Each cell tracks its own multiplier (×1 → ×2 → ×4 → … → ×999). Multipliers stay and grow throughout a session
  • Gold Spins bonus — Land 3, 4, or 5 BONUS symbols to trigger 6, 8, or 10 free spins where multipliers carry over between spins
  • Special symbols — STEAM upgrades a random multiplier, DRAIN clears the board and maximises one cell, FLOATER keeps one symbol across cascades, WILD substitutes for any symbol
  • Bonus Buy menu — Skip the wait and buy directly into bonus rounds or feature spins. Cards show the exact cost. Click a card to queue it for the next spin, or hit Buy & Spin to fire immediately
  • Auto-spin — Set 5, 10, 25, 50, or 100 auto-spins. Auto-spin pauses automatically when the bonus triggers and resumes only after you collect your winnings
  • Session stats — Track your spins, total wagered, total won, and best win right on the game screen

Blackjack & Case Battle

New game: Blackjack

Classic blackjack is here. Place your bet, get dealt two cards, and aim for 21. Hit to draw more, stand to hold, double down or split when the odds are right. Dealer follows standard rules (hit on 16 or less, stand on 17). Insurance and surrender available. Provably fair with server-side deck handling.

  • Standard rules — Dealer stands on 17, hits on 16 or below
  • Actions — Hit, Stand, Double Down, Split, Surrender
  • Insurance — When dealer shows an Ace
  • Animations — Sequential deal, dealer hole card flip, smooth card reveals

New game: Case Battle

Open cases against other players or bots. Pick a case, set the format (1v1, 1v1v1, 2v2, etc.), and create a battle. Everyone opens the same cases; the highest total value wins the pot. Multiple modes: Standard (highest total wins), Terminal (last round only), Co-op (split the pot), Jackpot (win chance based on your total).

  • Formats — 1v1, 1v1v1, 1v1v1v1, 2v2, 2v2v2, 3v3
  • Modes — Standard, Terminal, Co-op, Jackpot; Crazy mode toggle (lowest wins) for all except Co-op
  • Custom cases — Owners and admins can create cases with custom items and RTP
  • Pot distribution — Payouts based on total value of items opened, not entry price

Challenge system

Challenge other players to duels. Visit a profile, hit Challenge, choose your wager and game, then send. The challenged player gets an invite and can accept or decline. Everyone sees the challenge in chat.

  • Gamble lock — When you lose a challenge with gamble lock wager, you can’t place bets on any game until the lock expires. Custom toast instead of browser alert

  • Loser in a challenge sees the final result when the winner submits (no more “Waiting for opponent…”)

  • Mines: bet and autobet now respect gamble lock
